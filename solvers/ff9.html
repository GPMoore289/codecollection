Final Fantasy IX Skipping & Running

Original Creation: 11th December 2023

Uploaded: 4th March 2025

Status: Complete. Original code was written without AI.

Description: On a replay of Final Fantasy IX, I decided it was finally time to get two awards I'd never succeeded at before: King of Jump Rope and Athlete Queen. Problem is that both of these are very punishing on your thumbs and I'd rather not get carpal tunnel syndrome. Python to the rescue! This was an exercise in getting Python to interface with another program and actually manipulate it, rather than just producing an output in the IDLE shell, plus for these challenges, button presses had to be timed quite accurately.

print("Starting")

print("Go")
