Film Rankings

Description: Throughout the year, I keep a journal of new films and rank them at the end of the year from lowest to highest, then post them to social media. I deliberately do not number them, as inserting a new film in the middle would then require renumbering all films ranked lower than it. Instead, I order the films with a unique combination of characters (*#*) at the start of the line, and let this program automatically count and number them. Below, you can see an example from 2022. Hopefully, you approve of my ratings!

Original Creation: 29th November 2022

Uploaded: 25th March 2025

Status: Complete. Original code was written without AI.

Supplemental: Text file:example film list.

text = open("film.txt", "r")
reviews = text.read()
text.close()

inum = reviews.count("*#*")

while inum > 0:
    reviews = reviews.replace("*#*", str(inum), 1)
    inum -=1
    continue

new = open("reviews.txt", "w")
new.write(reviews)
new.close()
