Status: Incomplete, somewhat functional.

Description: At my girlfriend's family reunion, her cousin's daughter asked me how I was related to her. As someone with a small family, I never paid much attention to the naming system for distant family members, and I couldn't answer her question. Coupled with the fact that I find my girlfriend's family tree hard to navigate, it prompted me to create a family tree program. This was the first time I really utilized classes in a program, which made the inter-generational navigation much easier to manage. I paused this project as there were a lot of edge cases I hadn't accounted for, plus I had to get ready for college in a few weeks. Names have been changed for privacy reasons.
